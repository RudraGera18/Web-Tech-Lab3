Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook bacon in skillet until crispy.
  3. Remove bacon from skillet and set aside.
  4. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Place one slice of bread butter-side down in skillet.
  6. Add cheese and cooked bacon on top of bread in skillet.
  7. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  8. Cook until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted, flipping sandwich halfway through cooking.
  9. Remove sandwich from skillet and slice in half.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
